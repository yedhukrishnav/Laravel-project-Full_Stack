WELCOME TO YOUR RELAXATION PARADISE

BLEU Beach Resort's Experiences in Goa

Get ready for an unforgettable vacation at our resort with experiences in Goa. Each month we offer our guests a unique selection of different experiences. With our experts as your personal guides, we invite you to explore Azaya and Goa as each moment comes alive.

Discover Adventures Spa Sports Party

Discover the island

BLEU is a gateway to extraordinary expeditions on land or by sea. From horseback rides on the sand and kiteboarding along the coastline to nature hikes and scuba safaris, these island shores offer all flavours of adventure.

Explore the coastline

Providenciales’ Atlantic shores are ideal for watersports. Kayaks, stand-up paddleboards and Hobie Cats are always available. Between May and September, the breeze at Long Bay Beach creates perfect kiteboarding conditions. Scuba dive and snorkel extraordinary reefs – the magnificent coral and wall formations offer diving opportunities for all levels and private charter dives can be easily arranged.

Restoring the reef

Spend time at the Nature Discovery Centre for an insight into the beauty and balance of the undersea ecosystem. Embark on an eye-opening adventure in the world below the waves, and get a crash course in marine biology, learning how corals are the cornerstones of ocean life. In partnerships with Sustainable Oceans

Adventure for all ages

BLEU’s Nature Discovery Centre curates exciting experiences for children, teens and adults, from exploring rock pools to kayaking and snorkelling expeditions. From nature walks to energetic beach sports, BLEU’s offerings extend to the whole family. Enjoy a host of activities together, or relax while children participate in nature discovery, creative arts or sports programmes under the supervision of specially trained staff.

New to BLEU this year, and in association with the Phillip and Patricia Frost Museum of Science in Miami, discover a new set of programmes created to cultivate enthusiasm for the environment and passion for conservation amongst the next generation.

Private Yacht Charters

Experience the full freedom of the ocean on a private charter. BLEU has access to a fleet of sailing vessels and luxury yachts of all sizes, allowing guests to arrange bespoke cruises around Turks & Caicos.Spend a day discovering the hidden coves and secret beaches of Providenciales’ coastline; take on bonefish, blue marlin and tuna on a fishing trip; or simply savour the sunset over champagne.

SPA

When you want to slow down the time and feel the purifying effects of your holiday on your body, our SPA center combines the exotic atmosphere of the Far East with Turkish traditions, allowing you to have a healthy and peaceful holiday.Our SPA center, which allows our guests to experience peace and pleasure together with its Turkish Bath , massage rooms , sauna and relaxation areas , offers opportunities for an unforgettable holiday

Sports

For our guests who do not want to stay away from sports activities while having fun on holiday, BLEU Hotel & Resort offers activities throughout the day.During your holiday, you can relax your mind and body by starting the day with yoga and aerobics classes in the garden.During the day , you can find the opportunity to do different sports activities such as table tennis , beach volleyball , football , basketball and tennis . In addition to activities such as billiards , surf lessons , parachute , jet-ski and banana water sports activities that will delight our adrenaline-loving guests will add unforgettable moments to your holiday. If you wish, you can also take diving lessons and go on a colorful journey towards the magical world of the underwater.

Party

The Party Clubs of Goa are the hottest destination for a night out. Thriving music, glitzy décor, international Djs, a range of affordable drinks and snacks draws visitors in huge numbers. Here are some of the best option of Goa: Club BLEU's at Benaulim Beach.
